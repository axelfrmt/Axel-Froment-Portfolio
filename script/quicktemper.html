    About QuickTemper

    QuickTemper is an energetic third-person top down hotline miami like where the character has to beat his ennemies to constantly gain adrenaline. Based on the movie HyperTension, you have to finish each level without dropping your adrenaline to 0. Now, go beat some asses !

    Features

    • Top down camera
    • Adrenaline constantly dropping
    • Hand-to-hand combat only
    • Power-up
    • Freaking bombs system
    • Score system
