    About Find The Exit

    Find The Exit is a retro inspired doom like shooter, packed with bubbles everywhere ! Created for 2025 Game Jam with the theme "Bubble". Old doom's player or just a young fan of retro games, try it and be ready to face an infinite number waves of ennemies.

    Features

    • Doomlike camera feeling (with bouncing movement).
    • Bubbles shoot.
    • FPS.
    • Hand to hand ennemies.
    • Procedural level.
    • In reality, no exit, just an infinite battle.
