    About CSR

    CSR is a 2.5D side-scrolling runner created in just 24 hours for RSE Lab Day. The objective is simple: avoid the "right" words to protect your company's integrity, while eliminating the "wrong" words that could have a negative impact.

    Breakdown

    • Production period: Over 2 days only
    • Theme: The game reflects the theme of RSE Lab Day by promoting the idea of creating a safe and respectful workplace. Players are tasked with preventing harmful elements such as racism, discrimination, and stressful behaviors from affecting the environment.
    • Concept: The game features 3D side-scrolling environments with 2D character animation. Controls are simple: just use the up and down keys to dodge positive words and collect negative ones.
    • Position: Game Designer, Animator
    • Score system: Your performance affects an employee happiness gauge, which ranges from 0% to 100%. Your goal? Keep your company's happiness as close to 100% as possible!
