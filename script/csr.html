    About CSR

    CSR is a 2.5D side-scrolling runner created in just 24 hours for the RSE Lab Day. The objective is simple: avoid the 'right words' to protect your company while eliminating the 'wrong words' that could have a negative impact.

    Features

    • Top down camera
    • Adrenaline constantly dropping
    • Hand-to-hand combat only
    • Power-up
    • Freaking bombs system
    • Score system
