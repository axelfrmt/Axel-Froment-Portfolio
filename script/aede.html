    About Neraïa: Echo of the abyss

    Neraïa: Echo of the abyss is an isometric 3D puzzle game in which the player plays as a subaquatique creature using the sound as a mechanism to do different type of task.

    Breakdown

    • Underwater world
    • Production Period: Over a year as a long side project in parallele with our studies sessions
    • Team: 4 game designers with one specialized in Level Design, another one in Narrative Design, another in Sound design and the last one in Programming.
    • Sound Gameplay: Most of the time, sound and music are used as a rythm gameplay fonction. In Neraïa, it is used for both the narration core progress and an impactfull key controlle rof the world. Player is able to manipulate sphere sound bubbles that create a sound wave that can interact with various mechanism in the world. Use this wave and all these interaction to open your path to progress in your journey in quest of yourself.
    • Narrative Driven Game: Neraïa is all about a journey of acceptance both of yourself and differences but also to find people with who you can be yourself. There are a lot of meaningfull aspects behind the journey of our heroine and various people with various life events can assimilate themselve to her. The music, the world, the journey and its causes, it makes sense and can be affiliated with anyone.

    Development Summary

    Neraïa has not always be Neraïa, it was refered as Aede for a long time. It contains many refonts and dramastically big changes overtime. To put it simply, the narration, the gameplay, the environment, most of it have been completely changed from what is was at the end of our first chekpoint.
